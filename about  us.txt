                  <p>Government Women’s Polytechnic, Patna is located in Bihar. The education institution was established in the year 1985. The College was affiliated to State Board of Technical Education, Patna.It is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). Government Women’s Polytechnic , Patna offers various diploma courses. The college has a well developed infrastructure and facilities like computer lab, Auditorium, Sports and more. The college has well trained and experienced faculty members.
